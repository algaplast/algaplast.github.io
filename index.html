About Us

AlgaPlast is an innovative, eco-friendly bioplastic solution designed to replace conventional petroleum-based plastics. Utilizing a renewable algae-based formulation, it offers a biodegradable and sustainable alternative that reduces environmental impact while maintaining high mechanical strength and water resistance. Ideal for industrial, agricultural, and packaging applications, AlgaPlast is derived from Gracilaria Salicornia and Ulva Lactuca, ensuring a low-energy production process with minimal chemical use. Its high biodegradability supports circular economy efforts, making it a viable large-scale commercial alternative to traditional plastics. With a strong focus on sustainability and innovation, AlgaPlast represents a groundbreaking step toward a greener future, enabling industries to transition to more responsible and environmentally friendly materials.
